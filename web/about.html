About Web-App

Our web-app helps you to order food in the maximam efficient way possible in the Mocha restaurant.

Our software is self-contained and can be used to improve scenario in restaurants.

Our app works as per following steps.

  • All customer needs to do is enter the table number he/she is sitting at and a digital menu will be displayed on the screen from which customer can enter the food items along with quantity he/she want to enter.
  • Now he/she will be provided with a smart/digital menu. Customer will select required items from menu card along with quantity as per his/her choice for placing order.
  • This order will be transferred to ‘order attendant’ along with table information to a separate digital system provided with him. The information will be directed to chef .
  • To notify that customer is served and consumed completely, he/she may press a button provided and then bill is generated and customer can now select different modes of payment.
  • According to customer’s mode of payment, ‘payment collector’ will come and payment will be done.
  • Once you're done, your bill will be genrated and a payment collector will come and collect your payment.
